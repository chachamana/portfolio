Design Setting

Utilised deep green as the main corporate colour, with ochre as the secondary colour.
All illustrations were colour-adjusted to suit each content, ensuring a cohesive look across the entire site.

Following the design plan, crafted a simple yet trustworthy and warm website.

conducted a revamp to encourage navigation within the site.
